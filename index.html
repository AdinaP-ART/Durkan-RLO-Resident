Common questions
How do I confirm my appointment?
Go to Visits and select your preferred date. Once confirmed it is locked — contact your RLO to make changes.
How do I report a defect?
Tap the Defects tab. Add a description, photo and priority. Your RLO will be notified immediately.
What are site working hours?
Mon–Fri 8:00am–5:00pm. Out-of-hours work will be notified in advance.
What if none of the dates suit me?
Select "None of the above" — your RLO will arrange alternatives within 48 hours.
