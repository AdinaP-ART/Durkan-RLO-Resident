Common questions
How do I confirm my appointment?
Go to My Appointments and select your preferred date. Once confirmed it cannot be changed without contacting your RLO.
Can I change my confirmed date?
Once confirmed, dates are locked. Please message your RLO via the Message screen to request a change.
What are site working hours?
Mon–Fri 8:00am–5:00pm. Out-of-hours work will be notified in advance.
What if none of the dates suit me?
Select "None of the above" — your RLO will arrange alternatives within 48 hours.
