Common questions
How do I log in?
Scan the QR code on your welcome letter with your phone camera — it will log you in automatically. Or visit the app link and enter your access code.
How do I confirm my appointment?
Go to Pre Works Visits and select your preferred date. Once confirmed it is locked — contact your RLO to make changes.
What are site working hours?
Mon–Fri 8:00am–5:00pm. Out-of-hours work will be notified in advance.
What if none of the dates suit me?
Select "None of the above" — your RLO will arrange alternatives within 48 hours.
